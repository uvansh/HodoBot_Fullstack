Japan Travel Guide

Capital: Tokyo
Best Time to Visit: March-May (cherry blossoms) or September-November (fall colors)
Currency: Japanese Yen (JPY)
Language: Japanese, limited English in rural areas

Top Cities:
- Tokyo: Modern metropolis with tech and tradition
- Kyoto: Ancient temples and traditional culture
- Osaka: Food paradise and vibrant nightlife
- Hiroshima: Peace memorial and history

Visa: Visa-free for many countries (up to 90 days)
Budget: $70-100 per day (budget), $150-250 (mid-range)

Must Try: Sushi, Ramen, Tempura, Matcha desserts
Transportation: JR Pass recommended for tourists
